Our Policies

We understand that schedules change. To ensure we can accommodate all our clients, we require:

  • 24-hour notice for cancellations or rescheduling
  • Cancellations made with less than 24 hours notice will incur a 50% cancellation fee
  • No-shows will be charged the full appointment fee
  • Emergency situations will be handled on a case-by-case basis

To cancel or reschedule, please call us at (123) 456-7890 for Downtown Studio or (987) 654-3210 for Suburban Retreat.

Your Privacy Matters

Tough Love Massage is committed to protecting your personal and health information in compliance with GDPR and HIPAA regulations.

Information We Collect
  • Name, contact information, and appointment details
  • Medical history and health information relevant to your massage therapy
  • Payment information (processed securely through encrypted channels)
How We Use Your Information
  • To provide personalized massage therapy services
  • To communicate about appointments and services
  • To maintain accurate health records for continuity of care
  • To process payments securely
Data Security

All sensitive information is stored securely in encrypted databases. We never share your personal or health information with third parties except as required by law or with your explicit consent.

Your Rights
  • Access to your personal data
  • Right to correction of inaccurate information
  • Right to deletion of your data (subject to legal requirements)
  • Right to data portability

For privacy inquiries, contact us at privacy@toughlovemassage.com

Your health and safety are our top priorities:

  • All therapists are licensed and certified professionals
  • Equipment and linens are sanitized between each client
  • Please inform us of any health conditions, injuries, or allergies
  • Arrive 10 minutes early for first-time appointments to complete intake forms
  • If you feel unwell, please reschedule your appointment

  • Gift cards are valid for 2 years from purchase date
  • Gift cards are non-refundable but transferable
  • Lost or stolen gift cards can be replaced with proof of purchase
  • Gift cards can be used at both Downtown Studio and Suburban Retreat locations
  • Remaining balance stays on the card for future use
